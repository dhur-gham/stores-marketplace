## üìä Priority Recommendations

### High Priority (Customer Impact)
1. **Customer Profile Page** - Essential for account management
2. **Saved Addresses** - Improves checkout experience significantly
3. **Order Cancellation** - Customers need control over their orders
4. **Advanced Search & Filters** - Critical for product discovery
5. **Product Reviews** - Builds trust and helps customers

### High Priority (Store Owner Impact)
1. **Revenue Analytics** - Store owners need to see their financials
2. **Order Export** - Essential for accounting and record keeping
3. **Low Stock Dashboard Widget** - Critical for inventory management
4. **Product Export/Import** - Needed for bulk operations
5. **Customer List for Store** - Store owners should see their customers

### Medium Priority
1. Invoice downloads
2. Order tracking improvements
3. Product variants
4. Review management
5. Bulk product operations

### Low Priority (Nice to Have)
1. Loyalty programs
2. Gift options
3. Product comparison
4. Referral programs
